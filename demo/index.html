Shipping & Delivery

Terms of Use

This site is provided to showcase products, support customers, and process requests securely.

  • No abuse: Do not attempt to disrupt, scrape, or compromise the site.

Privacy & Customer Communication

Loe‑Life uses customer information only to operate the store: fulfill orders, provide support, and send transactional updates.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • Passwords are submitted securely to create your account and are not stored in page code or local storage.
  • Email & phone are used for receipts, shipping updates, and support responses.
  • SMS is optional and requires explicit opt‑in. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Data minimization: We collect only what we need to fulfill orders and support you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind Loe‑Life Apparel?

Loe‑Life is a veteran‑owned company founded by Dylan & Tracie Stevens. The brand was created to reflect resilience, discipline, and strength, both physical and mental.

What kind of apparel do you offer?

Performance-focused apparel and lifestyle pieces designed for training, everyday wear, and mental toughness.

How do I stay updated on new releases?

Follow Loe-Life on social platforms or check the Shop for new drops and updates.

How do I create a profile?

Use the Profile button at the top — we’ll collect shipping and billing addresses so checkout is faster.
